Identified the need for unified business intelligence across executive, operational, and customer-facing teams. Architected a comprehensive analytics platform that aggregated data from disparate enterprise systems into actionable real-time dashboards.

<h4>Strategic Value</h4>
* Enabled data-driven decision making across all organizational levels
* Unified fragmented data sources into single source of truth
* Provided executive leadership with real-time operational visibility
* Supported call center optimization through live performance metrics
* Established scalable integration patterns across enterprise data ecosystem
